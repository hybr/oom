V4L (Vocal 4 Local) - Geo-Intelligent Marketplace Platform Specification
Executive Overview
V4L (Vocal 4 Local) is a location-based marketplace platform connecting people, organizations, goods, and services through intelligent geographic discovery. Users can find, offer, and manage resources based on proximity, relevance, and community needs.

1. Platform Architecture
1.1 Core Modules

My Profile: Personal management (profile, education, skills, preferences)
Organization: Business/institutional management (branches, teams, positions)
Market: Goods & services marketplace with geo-intelligence
Common: Shared reference data and utilities
